Your Payoff in Part I

After you have completed all 40 rounds in Part One, the computer will randomly select one out of the 40 auctions, with each auction being equally likely to be selected. Your earnings from the selected auction will correspond to your payoff in Part One. You should therefore in each of the 40 auctions choose your bid carefully as if it were the one that will determine your payoff.

Your Payment in the Experiment

You will see your payment only at the end of the experiment, that is after having completed Part One and Part Two. The payment you will receive at the end of the experiment will be a combination of payoffs you earned in Part One (50%) and Part Two (50%). Your payoff in credits will be converted into dollars with an exchange rate of $1 for 6 credits. In addition, you will receive a participation fee of $10 and an endowment of $12. Negative earnings (losses) will be subtracted from your endowment of $12.

Hence, your payment will be calculated as follows:

Payment = $10 participation fee + $12 endowment + 0.5 ∗ Payoff in Part One (in $) + 0.5 ∗ Payoff in Part Two (in $)

You have now completed the instructions for Part I. Please send the experimenter a message if you have a question. Otherwise click on the button “NEXT" to complete a short comprehension quiz.
