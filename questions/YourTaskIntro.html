Guess the worth of the lottery. You will earn C12 if your guess is sufficiently close to the actual worth of the lottery; and nothing if it is too far away. How close your guess is to the actual worth regulates your chance of getting C12: The further away your guess is from the actual worth of the lottery, the lower is the probability that you get C12.

It can be shown that the best you can do is to state what you truly believe the answer to be.
