Now choose the smallest interval for which you are certain that it contains the estimated worth of the lottery if you had the highest signal. Just as before, the smaller your chosen interval is, the more you will earn (with a maximum of C12), but you will only earn a payoff if the correct estimate lies within your chosen interval.

For example, given the payoff formula we use, if you choose a narrow interval from 1 to 2, and the correct estimate is indeed between 1 and 2, then you would get C11 (this may slightly vary with the type of lottery).
