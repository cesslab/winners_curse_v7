In this question we are asking you to estimate the probability that the signal you saw is the highest of four independently drawn signals. To do this choose a number between 0% and 100%. Your answer will determine the probability that you will get a fixed payoff of C24. You increase your chance of getting C24, 1) the closer your number is to 100%, and you are indeed seeing the highest signal, or 2) the closer your number is to 0%, and you are NOT seeing the highest signal. The best you can do is to report your true belief about the probability.
