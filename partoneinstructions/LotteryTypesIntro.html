There will be four different types of lotteries where a lottery type is defined by a {{ player.selected_value_text }} and a probability of receiving that {{ player.selected_value_text }}. For each type of lottery, the computer will produce independently of each other 3 different lottery tickets and in each round you will see one of them. Moving from round to round will involve a change in the Selected Value holding the range of possible {{ player.selected_values_text }} and the probability fixed, while a new type of lottery may involve a change in both the range of {{ player.selected_values_text }} and the probability.
